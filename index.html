About me:

I have a lot of hobbies, I play the piano, and will probably get into guitar soon, a few of my favorite video game series would be the Ezio storyline of Assassin's Creed, the Portal & the Half-Life franchise by Valve and games like Sekiro & Bloodborne from FromSoft. I also love reading, anything from non-fiction (productivity books to history, etc.) to fiction (genres I enjoy would be fantasy, mystery, psychological, magical-realism), (though these days I'm mostly reading fiction.) Currently, I'm reading The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle by Haruki Murakami (I would recommend this if you want a story with well written characters in a setting there is very realistic with magical surrealism sprinkled on top.) & the Mistborn collection by Brandon Sanderson (I highly recommend it if you like LOTR or The Hobbit.)

I'm very passionate about learning about new technologies, I'm particularly interested in 2 fields right now and aspire to work & contribute to these in the future:

  • Machine Learning and how AI models are trained, especially the new LLM models like ChatGPT and other ones like Mid-Journey (for images) and the new ones that are now able to create audio and videos. I'm also pretty interested in self-driving cars.
  • Quantum Computing - I'm interested to see much would these improve in the subsequent years. I remember reading about these in high school that they use the concept of superposition and entanglement to process data in a much faster way than the way traditional computers do using binary.
